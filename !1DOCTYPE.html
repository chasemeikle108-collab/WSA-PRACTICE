University of Washington
Writing Skills Assessment

Practice both writing tasks under timed, exam conditions. No autocorrect. No distractions. Just you and the page.

Test Structure

  • 90 minutes total for both tasks
  • Persuasion Task (~55 min suggested)
  • Position Task (~35 min suggested)
  • No dictionaries or calculators
  • No specific knowledge required

Persuasion Task

  • Read a workplace scenario
  • Choose a recommended action
  • Write a persuasive letter
  • Analyze both numerical & verbal data
  • Address objections/counterarguments

Position Task

  • Choose one of two statements
  • Explain the statement in your words
  • Accept, reject, or alter it
  • Use your own examples
  • Address a general audience

Scoring

  • Each task scored 1–6 separately
  • Final score = average of both
  • Example: 3 + 4 = 3.5 overall
  • Score 0 = off-topic or illegible
  • Graded on separate rubrics
